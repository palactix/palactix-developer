# Introduction

Palactix is a unified API for social media publishing where agencies and developers bring their own OAuth apps.

Unlike traditional tools where client connections belong to the vendor, Palactix uses **your OAuth applications**. Tokens are issued to your app, not ours. You own the client relationships.

**Supported Platforms:** Meta (Facebook), Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X (Twitter), Youtube

---

## The Core Difference

**Traditional tools:**
```
Agency → Hootsuite → Client's Instagram
         ↑
    (Hootsuite owns token)
```

**Palactix:**
```
Agency → Your Meta App → Client's Instagram
         ↑
    (You own token)
```

**Result:** Switch tools anytime without reconnecting accounts.

---

## Why Palactix?

* **Zero Vendor Lock-in:** Since tokens belong to your App ID, you can move your infrastructure without asking clients to re-authenticate.
* **Sovereign Reputation:** Your publishing history is isolated to your own developer apps. You don't share a "Risk Pool" with other users.
* **Unified Interface:** One API to publish to Meta (Facebook/Instagram), LinkedIn, TikTok, X (Twitter), and Youtube.

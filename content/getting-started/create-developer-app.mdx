# Create Developer App

Before using Palactix APIs, you need to create a developer app.

This app represents your integration and is used to generate API credentials.

---

## Step 1: Open Developer Portal

Go to:

[Create your Developer App](/developer/apps)

---

## Step 2: Create a New App
![Create App Screen](/images/docs/docs-create-app.png)

Fill in the required details:

- **App Name** (required)  
- **Logo** (optional)  

Click **Create App**

---

## Step 3: Verify Your App

To activate your app, you must verify at least one platform.

Verification happens when you:

- Add platform credentials  
- Successfully connect your own account via OAuth  

Until then, your app remains in **draft mode**

---

## Step 4: Generate API Credentials

Once verified:

- You can generate your `client_id`
- You can generate your `client_secret`

⚠️ **Important:**
- The `client_secret` is shown **only once**
- Store it securely
- You can regenerate it later (old tokens will be revoked)

---

## What You Get

After setup:

- `client_id` → Public identifier  
- `client_secret` → Private credential  

These are used to generate API tokens.

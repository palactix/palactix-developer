# Configure Platform Credentials

Palactix uses a **Bring Your Own OAuth (BYO)** model.

You must provide your own platform credentials (App ID / Client ID / Secret).

---

## Why This Matters

With BYO OAuth:

- You own your client connections  
- Your app branding appears on consent screens  
- No vendor lock-in  
- Full control over permissions  

---

## Step 1: Create OAuth App on Platform

Create an app on the platform you want to use:

Examples:
- Meta (Facebook / Instagram)
- LinkedIn
- X (Twitter)
- TikTok

Each platform provides:

- `client_id`
- `client_secret`

---

## Step 2: Add Credentials in Palactix

Go to your app dashboard:

`/developer/apps/{app_id}`

Add credentials for a platform:

- Select platform  
- Enter `client_id`  
- Enter `client_secret`  

---

## Step 3: Verify Credentials

To verify:

1. Click **Connect Account**
2. Complete OAuth flow
3. If successful → platform marked as **verified**

---

## What Happens Internally

When verified:

- Credentials are stored securely  
- OAuth flow is enabled for that platform  
- Your app becomes **active**  

---

## Supported Platforms

- Instagram (via Meta)
- Facebook
- LinkedIn
- X (Twitter)
- TikTok

---

## Important Notes

- Credentials are encrypted at rest  
- Each app has isolated credentials  
- One app = one OAuth identity
